How to Use the ArkTrack System

Follow these steps to get started with the ArkTrack System:

  1. Step 1: Log In

    Visit the Home page and log in using your credentials. If you don't have an account, you can register from this page as well.

  2. Step 2: Set Up Your Security Preferences

    Navigate to the Dashboard to configure your security preferences. Set up the areas you want to monitor and adjust the sensitivity settings as needed.

  3. Step 3: Add Authorities

    Go to the Help page and follow the instructions to add the authorities you want to alert in case of suspicious activity. Ensure their contact details are accurate.

  4. Step 4: Monitor Your System

    Once your preferences are set, you can monitor live feeds and check recorded footage on the Dashboard. Ensure that your system is functioning correctly by reviewing the logs and notifications.

  5. Step 5: Respond to Alerts

    If an alert is triggered, you will receive a notification. Follow the instructions provided to respond promptly and ensure the security of your premises.

For further assistance, visit the Help page or contact our support team.
